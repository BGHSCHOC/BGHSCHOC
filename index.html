🆘 Help & Frequently Asked Questions

📍 Location Services Issues

Q: Why isn't my location working?

Safari Users: Safari often blocks location services. Try these solutions:

  • Use Google Chrome or Firefox instead
  • Go to Settings → Safari → Location Services → Website Settings
  • Make sure location is enabled for this website
  • Try refreshing the page and allow location when prompted

All Browsers: Make sure to click "Allow" when prompted for location access.

Q: I'm at the meeting but it says I'm not arrived?

This can happen due to GPS accuracy. Try:

  • Moving closer to the center of the meeting room
  • Going outside briefly to get better GPS signal
  • Refreshing your location by tapping "Check My Location"
  • Contact staff if the issue persists

📱 Sign-In Issues

Q: Why can't I sign in?

Sign-in may be disabled for several reasons:

  • Time Window: Sign-in is only open during meeting hours
  • Device Already Used: This device has already been used to sign in
  • Location Required: You need to be at the meeting location
  • Sign-in Closed: Staff may have temporarily disabled sign-in

Q: I already signed in but want to sign in again?

Each device can only be used once per meeting. Use a different device or contact staff to reset device sign-ins.

🔧 Technical Issues

Q: The page isn't working properly?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Contact staff if problems continue

Q: What browsers work best?

Recommended: Google Chrome, Firefox, Microsoft Edge

Limited Support: Safari (location issues common)

Not Recommended: Internet Explorer
